Quake

This event requires the group of participants to prepare a model of an earthquake resistant multi-storey framed structure of specific dimensions using readily available materials such as paper, fibre-board, strings, and cold liquid glue (fevicol).

The allowable materials to be used will be specified. The group of participants need to construct the model before hand and merely bring it to the event for testing. The models will be checked according to pre-set dimensions and then loaded with weights according to their floor areas, freeboards and self-weights. The model capable of withstanding the maximum amplitude on the shake table wins.
