Ro-Boats

A rarely heard event, ro-boats, is an event in which you have to make a floating robot to race on water paths. make a water resistant and compact floating robot to win this event. Wireless control of robot is recommended, the path will be introduced a few days prior to the event.

NOTE : WE WILL NOT BE RESPONSIBLE FOR THE DAMAGE TO YOUR ROBOT DURING THE EVENT DUE TO ANY REASON.

Rules/Instructions about Robot Dimensions ---

Height : 6 inch
Length(along the direction of its motion) : 10 inch
Width : 8 inch
Weight : Not Specific
Obstacles : Tunnel, Water Currents, Water Jet, Dloating Hurdles and few others.
Point of Doubt : The Propeller size should be such that it is included in these dimensions.
