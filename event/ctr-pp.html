Portal

It is an online event in which participants will be given a chance to register themselves online. Then they will be given a topic online post – registration, on the portal itself. They will need to formulate a report and submit it on portal for assessment within 25 days of registration but before the deadline.

A further addition to the portal would be online daily questions only answerable during post registration but prior to submission. They will be always in sync with the topic and will act as opportunities to gain points and get lead on the leader board. They’ll further act as new avenues to search for regarding the topic and make the report even more effective! The final results will be a combination of the question & answers and the report.
