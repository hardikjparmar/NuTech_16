Green-O-Plan

This event consists of three phases:
  1. Phase 1: An expert lecture on green building planning and the required materials and utilization of them.

  2. Phase 2: The participating groups will be given credit points at the start of the event.These credit points can be used to purchase various construction materials for walls, flooring, roofing, glass, energy savings etc. of different types in the form of an auction. All participating groups need to select a specific number of materials.

  3. Phase 3: The participants need to incorporate and utilize the materials selected, and draw a building plan (floor plan) according to pre-set requirements (number of bedrooms, washrooms, dining rooms, recreation areas etc.) and floor areas in the given time slot. Plans should highlight on the concepts of green buildings and sustainable construction practices.
