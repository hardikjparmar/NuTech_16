NU-Tech ever since its inception has grown from being a technical fest of Nirma university to being the Gujarat’s largest techno management colloquium.

Festival consists of technical events of all branches, workshops, paper presentation, non-technical events and fun events as well as IT-expo, Renowned speakers to motivate the youth and management events.

This year, NU-Tech aims at reaching out to all the colleges in India with a vision of spreading technology all over the country. College Representatives will play a crucial role in fulfilling this vision.

Reasons why you should become a College Representative

1. An opportunity to improve managerial skills

2. A chance to participate in and publicise various tech-related activities in your college

3. An opportunity to improve your communication skills while interacting with people coming from diverse fields and from various colleges across India

Responsibilities

Publicity

Putting up posters of NU-Tech'15 on college notice boards within 3 days of receiving poster.

Mailing

Forwarding the mails to the college mailing list

Media

Publicising the events of NU-Tech'15 on various social media including print media by sharing the links of the event

Coordination

Coordinating with the NU-Tech'15 Team in case any event is to be conducted in your college

Incentives

Several incentives are awaiting you with this responsibility

1. Certificate of OrganisationOn behalf of NU-Tech'15, IT-Nirma University.
2. College Representative CardA special card for proper recognition.
3. Best College Representative AwardA special Certificate by judging your work
